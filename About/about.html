Hotel History




The Hotel originally opened in 1864, borrowing its name from the charming expanse of ground referred to as the Sea Side Hotel, which stretches for a kilometer by the side of the Hotel, along the coast, where, in Victorian times, it had been a well-liked place to require a enter the ocean air, or a ride during a horse-drawn carriage. The Hotel was built section by section, the primary being the central area that results in the awe inspiring Ballrooms, followed by the South and North wings respectively.
